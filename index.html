UWM PLANETARIUM CLUB

ABOUT

UWM's Planetarium Club is a free organization for UWM students that meets twice a month during the school year. Here, students and planetarium staff experiment with club equipment such as our HTC VIVE virtual reality headset, starball projector, Uniview, watch past shows and go stargazing. There are also great volunteering opportunities!

MEETING TIMES

We gather times from every memeber and decide on a time that works best for everyone. We'll send out emails before each meeting with detials of what to expect!

JOIN US

The level at which you choose to be involved in the club is up to you, you may come to any or all of the meetings and participate however you feel suited.

To be a "registered" club member, you must be active in the club for at LEAST 5hrs/semester (this included meetings, volunteer hours, club organization, etc). Fully registered members will be allowed free admission to public planetarium programs.

We are always looking for volunteers and members to take initiative and take on a club role themselves to help organize the club and provide a leadership opportunity to members like you. If you are interested in a leadership role in the club, please see us at one of the previously mentioned meetings listed.
