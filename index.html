UWM PLANETARIUM CLUB

ABOUT

UWM's Planetarium Club is a free organization for UWM students that meets twice a month during the school year. Here, students and planetarium staff experiment with club equipment such as our HTC VIVE virtual reality headset, starball projector, Uniview, watch past shows and go stargazing. There are also great volunteering opportunities!

EVENTS

AUGUST 29: STARS N SMORES

Grab a free s’mores kit (1 per person) from the UWM Planetarium Club and roast them over a live fire. Then head inside for a live Planetarium show and learn how to identify constellations visible in the Milwaukee night sky. S’mores will be in the Physics courtyard if weather is permitting, otherwise it will be relocated indoors. Rooftop stargazing will be held separately after this event. Stickers available at the door!

OCTOBER 29, 30, 31: PLANESCARIUM

This Halloween join the UWM Planetarium for a spooktacular series of favorite scary movies projected on the planetarium dome. Our feature presentations include Hocus Pocus, IT, and Event Horizon. All shows will be free for UWM students! Stickers available at the door!
